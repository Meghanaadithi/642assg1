About Us

Welcome to the Computer Science Department at George Mason University. Our department is dedicated to delivering an outstanding education in the rapidly evolving field of computer science. We provide students with cutting-edge knowledge and hands-on experience to prepare them for successful careers in technology.

Our faculty comprises renowned experts in various areas of computing, from software engineering to artificial intelligence. We offer a diverse range of graduate programs designed to meet the needs of our students and the demands of the industry. Whether you are interested in deepening your knowledge or pursuing a specialized career path, our programs are tailored to help you achieve your goals and make a meaningful impact in the tech world.
